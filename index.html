Good to know

Questions, answered

What areas do you cover?
Mobile massage is available within roughly 20 minutes' drive of Blackheath — including Medlow Bath, Katoomba, Leura, Mount Victoria and the Megalong Valley, across the upper Blue Mountains, NSW.
Where does the massage take place?
Wherever you are. The service is fully mobile, so treatments happen in your own home, hotel, chalet or cottage. You provide the space; I bring the table, fresh linens and oils.
How long are the treatments?
Hands-on time runs from 30 to 120 minutes depending on the treatment. Please allow an extra 15 minutes either side for set-up, a short pre-massage consultation and packing up.
How much does a mobile massage cost?
The Relaxation Massage and The Foot Rub (both 60 min) are $150, The Signature Massage (90 min) is $195, and The Discovery Massage (120 min) is $240. A 30-minute foot add-on is $45. All prices are in AUD for mobile service within 20 minutes of Blackheath.
